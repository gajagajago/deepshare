Slurm User Group Meeting 2021
21 September 2020
Online
Host: SchedMD

The Slurm User Group Meeting (SLUG'21) this fall will be online again.

In lieu of an in-person meeting, SchedMD will broadcast a set of five presentations on Tuesday, September 21st, 2021 on our YouTube channel.

There is no cost to attend, and there is no registration required.
